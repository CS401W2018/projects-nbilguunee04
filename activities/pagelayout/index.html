MONGOLIAN HORSE RACE

Mongolian horse race is unique any other horse derby in the world. The horses are ridden by mostly children under 13 years old. Due to the long distance, average of 14 miles, and harsh weather condition including hot summer and cold winter, it is unique by its endurance and more speed.

In old times ,the race horses are ridden without saddles and children rode without any protective gears. However; due to increased child safety, riders nowadays use protective gears such as helmets, body shield and built body camera. The jockey must be protected by accident insurance.
