The best time to visit Mongolia is from May to early October, when the weather is warm and the landscapes are at their most vibrant. Summer, particularly July, is ideal for experiencing festivals like Naadam, exploring the lush steppes, or camping under clear skies. Early autumn offers cooler temperatures and stunning golden hues in the countryside, making it perfect for trekking and photography.
