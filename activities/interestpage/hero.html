Professional Dota 2 player Topson

Legendary player Topson started playing dota at age of 8. His professional career began in 2017 competing in lower division tournaments. However; it took him only one year to compete in S-tier tournaments. Despite low expectations at the beginning of the World champpionship, Topson and his team lifted the Aegies the cup after defeating their strong opponent. However; the story did not stop there, Topson made history by winning back to back the Aegies. Since and before, no team and player did back to back win at World Championship like him.
