What is the difference between a block and an inline tag? Give examples of each one.

Block tag takes up the full width available. Examples: div, h1, p.

What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal flow is block elements stack vertically and inline elements align horizontally within their container.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float allows elements to be positioned to the left or right of their container, allowing text elements wrap arount it. clear float is used to stop other elements from wrapping around the floated ones and make everything line up correctly.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

  • Relative positions the elements relative to its normal position.
  • Absolute positions elements exact at the pinned point.
  • Fixed positions the element to the browser window, staying fixed as the page scrolls such as navigation bar.
