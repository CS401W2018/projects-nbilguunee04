The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

Action attribute specifiec the URL where the form data will be sent.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

Method attribute determines how the form data is sent. The most common methods are "get" and "post".

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

GET appends form data to the URL, visible in the browser.
POST sends form data in the request body, used for actions that change data.
