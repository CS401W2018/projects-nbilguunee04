Here is the family page of Monkey D family including three generations of the family. All of three are has significant roles and considered as Monster for different situation and people in One piece world.

  • Strong
  • Leaders
  • Brave

Monkey D. Garp

Monkey D. Garp is Navy Vice-Admiral and the only one who is named as "The Navy Hero" in One Piece world. According his belief that Navy brings justice to this world and its his duty bring justice and protection to people, he is well respected by his comrades.

Monkey D. Dragon

He is commander of Revolunatory army. He is categorized as most wanted man in One piece world due his goal and action against World Government. Even though he has navy admiral father background, just like tribe "D" he choose his own way different than his father.

Monkey D. Luffy

He is the third generation of Monkey family and is on his way to become the Pirate King. Even his grandfather wanted and trained him to become navy soldier, Luffy himself choose to follow the road of Pirate.
