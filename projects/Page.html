The MongolZ

Professional E-sport organization

'The Mongolz' is known by their impressive success in Counter strike tournaments since 2023.
Consecutive wins over S Tier tournaments made them to stand #10 team in the World rank which made them second Asian team to stand at #10 in the Counter strike history.

Some of the achievements:

  • MESA Nomadic Masters Spring 2024 - 1st place- 50,000$
  • YaLLa Compass 2024 -1st place- 200,000$
  • Skyesports Championship 2024 -1st place- 70,000$

Impacts on Asian counter strike

  1. Increased Asian teams slot for High rank tournaments.
  2. One of the few Asian teams in history that made it to MAJOR tournaments.

Their achievements not only represented or stepped up Mongolian counter strike but Asian counter strike. Making themselves as A-tier to S-tier team took only half a year for them. In game leader Blitz is now known as all time #1 IGL in Asian counter strike history.
